ABOUT US

At Grocies Supermarket, we take pride in providing quality products and exceptional service to our customers across Sri Lanka. With multiple branches strategically located in key cities, including Bambalapitiya, Kadawatha, and Kandy, we aim to bring convenience and accessibility closer to you. Our Bambalapitiya branch, situated in the heart of Colombo, offers a wide range of groceries, fresh produce, household essentials, and more, catering to the diverse needs of urban dwellers and busy professionals.
In Kadawatha, our supermarket serves the thriving suburban community with a comprehensive selection of products, including locally sourced fruits and vegetables, imported goods, and specialty items, ensuring that residents have access to quality goods without having to travel far.
Nestled amidst the scenic hills of Kandy, our branch brings the same commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction to the residents of this historic city. With a focus on freshness and variety, we strive to be the preferred shopping destination for both locals and tourists alike.
At Grocies Supermarket, we are dedicated to exceeding your expectations and providing you with a seamless shopping experience. Whether you're stocking up on essentials, exploring new culinary delights, or simply enjoying the convenience of online shopping, we are here to serve you every step of the way.

Also, we are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and highest quality products sourced directly from trusted suppliers. Our vegetables come straight from the lush farms of Nuwara Eliya, where they are grown with care and dedication by local farmers. From vibrant greens to crisp root vegetables, each item is handpicked to ensure freshness and flavor, bringing the essence of nature to your table.
In our cosmetics section, we partner with renowned brands such as Dior, Revlon, and Spa Ceylon to offer you a wide range of beauty and skincare products. Whether you're looking for luxurious skincare treatments, trendy makeup essentials, or indulgent fragrances, our selection curated from leading brands promises to elevate your beauty routine to new heights. When it comes to dairy products, we take pride in our partnership with Kotmale Dairy, a trusted name in the industry known for its premium quality and freshness. From creamy milk to artisanal cheeses, each dairy product is carefully sourced and delivered straight from the pastures of Kotmale, ensuring that you enjoy nothing but the best with every purchase.
At Grocies Supermarket, we believe in supporting local farmers, fostering partnerships with renowned brands, and prioritizing freshness and quality above all else. With every visit, you can trust that you're not just shopping for groceries – you're embarking on a culinary journey filled with flavors, experiences, and the assurance of excellence.
