ABOUT OUR VEGETABLE SUPPLIERS




At Grocies Supermarket, we take pride in sourcing the freshest and highest-quality vegetables from diverse locations across Sri Lanka. Our vegetables are sourced directly from three main regions renowned for their fertile soils and optimal growing conditions



Nuwara Eliya Highlands

Nestled in the lush greenery of Sri Lanka's central highlands, Nuwara Eliya is renowned for its cool climate and fertile soil, making it an ideal location for cultivating a variety of vegetables. The crisp air and abundant rainfall create the perfect conditions for growing high-quality produce, including carrots, cabbage, broccoli, and lettuce. Our partnership with local farmers in Nuwara Eliya ensures that we source fresh, pesticide-free vegetables directly from the fields to our shelves, guaranteeing freshness and quality for our customers.

Dambulla Vegetable Market

Situated in the heart of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle, Dambulla is home to one of the largest wholesale vegetable markets in the country. Here, bustling stalls brim with a vibrant array of seasonal vegetables sourced from farms across the island. Our dedicated team of buyers carefully selects the finest produce, including tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, and green beans, ensuring that only the freshest and best-quality vegetables make their way to Grocies Supermarket. By supporting local farmers and traders in Dambulla, we uphold our commitment to offering our customers the freshest and most diverse selection of vegetables year-round.

Colombo Suburbs

In the bustling suburbs of Colombo, a network of small-scale farms and vegetable gardens flourishes amidst urban life. These local growers supply a wide range of fresh and exotic vegetables, catering to the diverse culinary preferences of our customers. From aromatic herbs and leafy greens to tropical delights like okra and snake gourd, our partnership with urban farmers in the Colombo suburbs ensures a steady supply of locally grown produce, reducing carbon emissions associated with transportation and supporting sustainable agriculture practices. At Grocies Supermarket, we take pride in promoting community-based agriculture and bringing the best of Sri Lanka's farm-fresh vegetables to your table.
